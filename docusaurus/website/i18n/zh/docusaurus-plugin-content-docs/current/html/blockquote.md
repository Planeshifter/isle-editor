显示扩展报价的组件。

## Options

* __cite__ | `string`: 出处. Default: `none`.
* __className__ | `string`: 班名. Default: `''`.
* __style__ | `object`: CSS内联样式. Default: `{}`.


## Examples

```jsx live
<blockquote>
After all is said and done, more is said than done.
</blockquote>
```
